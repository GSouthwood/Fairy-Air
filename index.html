Surprising friends and family with an unexpected visit home just got a lot simpler. Don't waste your time photoshopping flight itineraries, booking and canceling flights, or writing your own confirmation emails. Whether you want to surprise your loved ones by coming home early for a college break, or give your family the unexpected present of yourself on Christmas morning, Fairy Air will help you get there. All you have to do is input when, where, and who and your Fairy Air itinerary will be created.

How it works

Step one:

Buy a flight home for the actual date of your surprise arrival.

Step two:

Fill out the Fairy Air form with your name, where you are going, and the date and time of your fake arrival. Your fake itinerary will be created in seconds and you can send this to your unwitting family when they ask for your travel plans.

Step three:

Surprise your friends and family and share an experience that will stay with you forever.
